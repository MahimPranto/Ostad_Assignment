Rules and Regulations

1. Group stage: Total 10 minutes, 5 minutes per half.
Round of 16: Total 12 minutes, 6 min per half
Quarter Final: Total 14 minutes, 7 min per half
Semi-Final: Total 16 minutes, 8 min per half
Final: Total 20 minutes, 10 min per half.
2. A team failing to start the match with 7 players may begin the game with a minimum of 5 players, or "A Walkover" will be given. Theteam should be present 20 minutes before the scheduled time.
3. There will be a coin toss beforethe match for, Possession of the ball and Direction of attack.
4. The tournament will follow the rolling sub-method. Players may not enter the game until recognized by the referee.
5. The decision made by the referee will stand as the final decision.
6. Group stage and then knockout.
7. Points to becounted as follows in group stage matches:
WIN: 3 points
DRAW: 1 point
Ro16: If thescoreis tied, the result will be decided by penalties.
QF, SF & Final: Extra time will be given if thescoreis not settled. If thescoreis still tied, the result will be decided by penalties.
8. Two Yellow cards or a red card will result in a ban for the particular match and a ban for the next match.
9. Footwear is mandatory. No one will be allowed to play barefoot or spiked boots.
10. If anyoneis seen misbehaving with the referee, he will beshown a straight red card and can also be fined 500 takes.
11. Each team is required to bring 2 alternatesets of jerseys. The same Jerseyis needed.
12. NON-IUB STUDENTS are not allowed to play.
13. One player can register for oneteam only. An auction can take place after the Round of 16
14. Only Registered Players for the tournament can give their names in the Auction.
15. Smoking is strictly prohibited in the Sports Complex or area of the Sports Complex.
16. No team will not be allowed to participate without paying the entry fees.
17. BACKPASS IS NOT ALLOWED
P.S: Organisers hold supreme authority over anychanges in thiscompetition.
